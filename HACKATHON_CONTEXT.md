## Why This Matters for Swing Around

This chat is a live demonstration of the exact problem Swing Around solves: 235 people trying to find teammates in a group chat, posting walls of text with skills and interests, hoping the right person sees it.

Patterns visible in this chat:
- Solo people shouting into the void with LinkedIn links
- Formed teams with one gap they can't fill efficiently (Jenny + Ani looking for backend)
- Niche skill needs — neurotech, AI x CAD, agriculture, bundlers, CVE exploits — that are nearly impossible to surface in a flat chat
- People arriving at staggered times and missing earlier posts entirely
- No way to filter by skill, interest, or availability

The profiled participants above are a ready-made test dataset: real people, real skill tags, real matching needs. Notable signals for Swing Around's matching logic:
- Shashank Motepalli explicitly came to explore multi-agent infra with GBrain
- Aditya Advani came specifically to benchmark GBrain's relationship memory model
- Both are directly relevant to the product and are potential early users
