## Relevance to Swing Around
This chat is a **live demonstration of the exact problem Swing Around solves**: 235 people trying to find teammates in a group chat, posting walls of text with skills/interests, hoping the right person sees it. The patterns here:
- Solo people shouting into the void with LinkedIn links
- Formed teams with 1 gap they can't fill efficiently
- Niche skill needs (neurotech, AI x CAD, agriculture, bundlers) that are hard to surface in a flat chat
- People arriving at different times, missing earlier posts

The participant list above is a ready-made test dataset — real people with real profiles, real skill tags, and real matching needs. Shashank Motepalli (Ekai Labs) specifically called out wanting to explore "multi-agent infra with gbrain," and Aditya Advani (moltpod) explicitly said he wanted to benchmark GBrain's relationship memory model. Both are relevant to what you're building.
